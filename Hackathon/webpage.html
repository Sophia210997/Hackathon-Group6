YUSOBIYA GAMES

Whack-A-Mole

Ready for some fast-action, mole-whacking fun? Grab your curser and lets get started.

Snake

Snake is a classic game that requires players to assess their surroundings and find the quickest or safest route to a point. This is an excellent opportunity to learn about spatial awareness and plan ahead to your next move.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is an arcade-style game in which the player controls the bird Faby, which moves persistently to the right. The player is tasked with navigating Faby through pairs of pipes that have equally sized gaps placed at random heights.
